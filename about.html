Cloe Fan

I am a recent graduate from the Rhode Island School of Design with a degree in Textiles. I have always been fascinated by all facets of the art world but at RISD I have been honing my skills in knitting, weaving and print design. While studying at RISD, I broaden my interests in culture, craft and design in an effort to create thoughtful and engaging work. I grew up in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, where I developed a love of nature, animals (especially dogs), music, good food and travel.
