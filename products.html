Products


These experimental garments were created in my Sophomore and Junior year at RISD. The assignment was to create “Loom Based” garments, meaning they had to be made using geometric shapes. I created three garments only using circles and semi-circles to challenge my short-rowing skills. All the other garments were created using this mindset. I also got the opportunity to contribute to the Bjarne Melgaard X Eckhaus Latta show: Ignorant Transparencies in 2013, where I hand knitted two garments for the show. The Beetle Chair was a collaborative project, in my Senior year, with a friend in the Industrial Design program at RISD. This chair’s upholstery was inspired by the shape and color of beetles.
